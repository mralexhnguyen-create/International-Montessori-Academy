Our Approach

A philosophy rooted
in the whole child

Montessori education honors each child's natural drive to learn, explore, and contribute to the world around them.

I
🌱

Child-Led Discovery

Children choose their work within a carefully prepared environment, developing intrinsic motivation and deep concentration that lasts a lifetime.

II
🤝

Mixed-Age Community

Multi-age classrooms spanning 3-year spans foster mentorship, empathy, and leadership. Older students reinforce their learning by guiding younger peers.

III
🧠

Hands-On Materials

Montessori materials are specifically designed to make abstract concepts tangible, engaging all senses and accommodating every learning style.

Academic Programs

Programs for every
stage of childhood

From infant care through middle school, each program is thoughtfully designed around your child's developmental stage.

Ages 3 months – 18 months

Nido 🐣

A warm, nurturing environment for infants to develop movement, language, and sensory awareness at their own pace alongside responsive caregivers.

  • Low adult-to-infant ratio (1:3)
  • Freedom of movement
  • Natural materials and light
Ages 3 – 6

Children's House 🏡

The foundation of Montessori education. Children develop independence, language, early math, and grace in a beautifully prepared classroom environment.

  • Practical life & sensorial activities
  • Phonics and early literacy
  • Uninterrupted 3-hour work cycles
Ages 6 – 12

Elementary 🔬

The "going out" years. Students pursue academic mastery through research, collaboration, and community projects rooted in the Great Lessons framework.

  • Integrated humanities & sciences
  • Research and project-based learning
  • California state standards aligned
Ages 12 – 15

Middle School 🌍

Adolescent students build identity and purpose through Erdkinder principles: real-world experience, entrepreneurship, and deep academic challenge.

  • Micro-enterprise & community service
  • College preparatory coursework
  • Interdisciplinary project focus

How to Enroll

The path to IMACM is simple

As a public charter school, there are no tuition fees. Every family is welcome to apply.

1

Request Information

Use the button in the corner or fill out our short inquiry form. Our enrollment team will contact you within 2 business days.

2

Attend a Campus Tour

See our prepared environments firsthand. Tours are held on the first and third Tuesday of each month at 9:00 a.m.

3

Complete the Application

Submit your application online or in person. If the program is at capacity, your child is placed on our waitlist in order of application date.

4

Accept Your Offer

Once accepted, return your enrollment agreement and required documents. Welcome to the IMACM family!
